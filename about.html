ARS TECHNOLOGIES

ARS Tech established in 2005, is a leading technology-driven company based in Telangana, India. We are the Sole Proprietorship based company that is involved in the manufacturing, wholesaling, manufacturing, and trading of Wireless-Water Level controllers and Home Protectors, Water Pump controllers Mobile starters, and many more. The main objective of our company is to provide a complete solution for water management automation systems for the domestic, industrial, and agricultural sectors. We are one of the leading automatic water pump controller manufacturing companies, We are also pioneers in bringing mobile-based automatic water pump controllers and starters which has made a tremendous impact in the agricultural sector. We also provide customized products such as automatic water pump controllers for residential houses, apartments, multiplex, hospitals, hotels, schools, colleges, and many other places and customized timers for hoarding lights and street lights. The company has also provided solutions to automation in drip irigation system mobile based on the revolution in the agriculture field. The company has its technology center with dynamic young aspirants thriving and willing to provide solutions to the challenging problems encountered
